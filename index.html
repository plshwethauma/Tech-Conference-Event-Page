NeoTech Horizons 2025

November 14–16, 2025 • Bangalore International Center, Domlur, Bangalore, India

A future-focused technology summit bringing together innovators, engineers, researchers, and entrepreneurs to explore breakthroughs in AI, quantum computing, sustainable tech, and human-technology integration. The event focuses on practical applications rather than just theory, attendees leave with real tools, contacts, and next-step ideas.

Keynote Speakers

Mike Krieger

Co-founder of Instagram, UX Designer, and Engineer with insights into building innovativee platforms.

Padmasree Warrior

Former CTO of Motorola & Cisco, award-winning technologist, and advocate for women in STEM.

Martha Lane Fox

Co-founder of Lastminute.com, UK Digital Champion, and influential leader in digital transformation.

Dag Kittlaus

Co-creator of Siri & Viv Labs, visionary in voice AI and digital innovation.
